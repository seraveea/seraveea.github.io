Estimation, Comparison and Analysis of Yellow Taxi in NYC

Around 8 gigabyte data was used to train a machine learning model to generate the taxi fare in NYC by pick up location and destination. An interactive user web page was built which can show the map of NYC by Google map and the price could be compute back-end. Several experiments were made to analysis and compare the price of Lyft and yellow taxi.

Keywords: machine learning, data cleaning, data analysis, data visualization
